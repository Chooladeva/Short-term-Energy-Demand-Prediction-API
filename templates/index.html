Energy Demand Prediction in Australia

Short-term energy demand predictions for Tasmania, New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia, and Queensland based on weather, seasonal, and temporal parameters

Note: The seasons in Australia are at opposite times to those in the Northern Hemisphere (Summer from December to February, Autumn from March to May, Winter from June to August, and Spring from September to November). The times of the day are categorized as Morning (05–12), Afternoon (12–17), Evening (17–21), and Night (21–05) in 24-hour time.
